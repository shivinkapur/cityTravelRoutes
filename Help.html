How do I get started?
The first thing you need to do for accessing the main functions of the website is to register yourself with us. Registration is absolutely free and it should not take more than 5 minutes of your time. After registration we send a confirmation e-mail to the specified e-mail address.On confirmation you can start using our website.

What after registration?
After completing your registration you are free to log-in using the email id and password specified by you during registration. After you login a map of Mumbai will appear and you can select the source area and the destination area. After doing so click on the submit button which will re-direct you to a new page with more specific details of your selected area.Once you select the sub-areas click on submit to view your result.

What details are provided about each route?
For each route you are provided with the bus numbers, nearest station, train time-table as well as approximate auto-rickshaw and taxi fare along with the time required to reach the destination. We also provide you with a snapshot of the route.

What else can the web-site be used for?
In addition to finding out the route our website also provides information about traffic snags in the city, train delays and any other problems you may face while travelling around in the city. We also have a downloads section which can be used for downloading concession forms, application forms,etc. In addition to this we have a 24 hr helpline for emergencies only. .
